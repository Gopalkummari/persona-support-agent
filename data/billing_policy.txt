BILLING POLICY AND PROCEDURES
==============================

Last Updated: January 2024
Document Version: 3.2

1. BILLING CYCLE OVERVIEW
-------------------------
All customer accounts are billed on a monthly cycle. The billing date is determined 
by the account creation date. For example, if your account was created on March 15th, 
your billing cycle runs from the 15th of each month to the 14th of the following month.

Invoices are generated automatically 3 business days before the billing date. 
Customers receive an email notification with the invoice summary and a link to 
download the detailed PDF invoice from their dashboard.

2. PAYMENT METHODS
------------------
We accept the following payment methods:
- Credit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
- Debit Cards: Visa Debit, MasterCard Debit
- ACH Bank Transfer (US accounts only, minimum $500 balance)
- Wire Transfer (Enterprise accounts only)
- PayPal (Pro and Enterprise plans)

To update your payment method:
1. Log in to your account dashboard
2. Navigate to Settings → Billing → Payment Methods
3. Click "Add Payment Method" or "Edit" on an existing method
4. Enter the new payment details and click "Save"
5. Set the new method as default if desired

4. REFUND POLICY
----------------
We offer refunds under the following conditions:

a) 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee:
   - Available for first-time subscribers only
   - Must be requested within 30 days of initial purchase
   - Full refund of the subscription amount
   - Does not apply to add-on purchases or overages

b) Service Outage Credits:
   - If uptime falls below our 99.9% SLA guarantee
   - Credits are calculated proportionally to downtime duration
   - Credits are applied automatically to the next billing cycle
   - Maximum credit per month: 30% of monthly subscription fee

c) Billing Error Corrections:
   - Duplicate charges are refunded within 5-7 business days
   - Unauthorized charges investigated within 48 hours
   - Contact billing@support.example.com with transaction ID

d) Refund Processing Times:
   - Credit Card: 5-10 business days
   - Bank Transfer: 10-15 business days
   - PayPal: 3-5 business days

5. DUPLICATE CHARGE DISPUTES
-----------------------------
If you notice duplicate charges on your statement:

Step 1: Log into your dashboard and go to Billing → Transaction History
Step 2: Identify the duplicate transaction IDs
Step 3: Click "Dispute" next to the duplicate charge
Step 4: Fill in the dispute form with:
   - Transaction ID of the original charge
   - Transaction ID of the duplicate charge
   - Brief description of the issue
Step 5: Submit the dispute. Our billing team reviews within 48 hours.
Step 6: You will receive an email confirmation once the refund is processed.

For urgent billing issues, contact our billing hotline: +1-800-555-BILL (2455)
Available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST.

6. INVOICE MANAGEMENT
----------------------
To download invoices:
1. Go to Dashboard → Billing → Invoices
2. Select the date range
3. Click the download icon next to any invoice for PDF export
4. Use "Export All" for bulk CSV download of transaction history

For custom invoicing (Enterprise plans):
- Contact your account manager
- Provide your company's tax ID / VAT number
- Specify any required PO numbers or custom fields

7. CANCELLATION POLICY
-----------------------
To cancel your subscription:
1. Navigate to Settings → Subscription → Cancel Plan
2. Select a reason for cancellation (optional but appreciated)
3. Confirm cancellation

Important notes:
- Your access continues until the end of the current billing period
- Data is retained for 90 days after cancellation
- After 90 days, all data is permanently deleted
- You can reactivate within 90 days without data loss
- Annual subscriptions: prorated refund available within first 60 days

8. TAX INFORMATION
-------------------
- US Customers: Sales tax applied based on state regulations
- EU Customers: VAT charged at applicable local rate
- VAT-exempt organizations: Submit exemption certificate to billing@support.example.com
- Tax invoices available for download from the billing dashboard
