UPTIME SLA AND SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
========================================

Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Document Version: 2.1

1. SERVICE AVAILABILITY GUARANTEE
----------------------------------
We guarantee 99.9% uptime for all paid subscription plans, measured on a 
monthly basis. This translates to a maximum of approximately 43 minutes of 
unplanned downtime per month.

Uptime Calculation:
   Uptime % = ((Total Minutes in Month - Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes in Month) × 100

Excluded from downtime calculations:
- Scheduled maintenance windows (announced 72 hours in advance)
- Force majeure events (natural disasters, government actions)
- Customer-side network or infrastructure issues
- Third-party service provider outages outside our control

2. SCHEDULED MAINTENANCE
--------------------------
Maintenance Window: Every Sunday, 2:00 AM - 6:00 AM UTC
- Advance notice: Minimum 72 hours via email and status page
- Expected duration: Typically 30-60 minutes
- Emergency patches: May be deployed outside the window with 4-hour notice

Status Page: https://status.example.com
- Real-time service status for all components
- Subscribe to updates via email, SMS, or RSS
- Historical uptime data available for the past 12 months

3. INCIDENT RESPONSE TIMES
----------------------------
| Severity  | Description                      | Response Time | Resolution Target |
|-----------|----------------------------------|---------------|-------------------|
| Critical  | Complete service outage           | 15 minutes    | 4 hours           |
| High      | Major feature unavailable         | 30 minutes    | 8 hours           |
| Medium    | Minor feature degraded            | 2 hours       | 24 hours          |
| Low       | Cosmetic issue or enhancement     | 8 hours       | 5 business days   |

4. SLA CREDITS
--------------
If monthly uptime falls below guaranteed levels:

| Monthly Uptime    | Service Credit (% of monthly fee) |
|-------------------|------------------------------------|
| 99.0% - 99.9%    | 10%                                |
| 95.0% - 99.0%    | 25%                                |
| Below 95.0%      | 50%                                |

To claim SLA credits:
1. Submit a credit request within 30 days of the incident
2. Include the dates and times of experienced downtime
3. Reference incident IDs from our status page
4. Credits are applied to the next billing cycle

5. MONITORING AND REPORTING
----------------------------
We provide the following monitoring capabilities:
- Real-time dashboard: Dashboard → Analytics → System Health
- Uptime reports: Automatically generated monthly, available for download
- Custom alerts: Configure threshold-based notifications for response times
- API endpoint: GET /v2/status returns current system health in JSON format

6. DISASTER RECOVERY
---------------------
- Data replicated across 3 geographic regions in real-time
- Recovery Point Objective (RPO): 1 hour maximum data loss
- Recovery Time Objective (RTO): 4 hours maximum to restore service
- Annual disaster recovery drills conducted and documented
- Backup verification performed daily with automated integrity checks

7. SUPPORT AVAILABILITY FOR OUTAGES
-------------------------------------
During service outages:
- Engineering team on-call 24/7
- Dedicated incident Slack channel created for Critical/High severity
- Customer updates posted every 30 minutes until resolution
- Post-incident report (RCA) published within 5 business days

For questions about our SLA, contact enterprise-support@example.com
