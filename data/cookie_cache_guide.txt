COOKIE AND CACHE CLEARING GUIDE
================================

If you are experiencing loading issues, display errors, or stale data in our 
web application, clearing your browser's cookies and cache is often the first 
recommended troubleshooting step.

WHY CLEAR COOKIES AND CACHE?
----------------------------
Cookies store session data, login tokens, and preferences. Over time, these 
can become corrupted or outdated, causing:
- Pages failing to load or displaying blank screens
- Login loops where you are repeatedly asked to sign in
- Outdated interface elements or missing features
- "Session expired" errors even after recent login

Cache stores copies of web assets (CSS, JavaScript, images) to speed up page 
loading. Corrupted cache files can cause:
- Broken layouts or missing styling
- JavaScript errors in the browser console
- Features not working after a platform update

CLEARING INSTRUCTIONS BY BROWSER
---------------------------------

Google Chrome:
1. Click the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner
2. Go to Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear Browsing Data
3. Set time range to "All time"
4. Check "Cookies and other site data" and "Cached images and files"
5. Click "Clear data"
6. Restart Chrome and log in again

Mozilla Firefox:
1. Click the hamburger menu (☰) in the top-right corner
2. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security
3. Scroll to "Cookies and Site Data"
4. Click "Clear Data"
5. Check both checkboxes and click "Clear"
6. Restart Firefox and log in again

Microsoft Edge:
1. Click the three-dot menu (…) in the top-right corner
2. Go to Settings → Privacy, Search, and Services
3. Under "Clear browsing data," click "Choose what to clear"
4. Select "Cookies and other site data" and "Cached images and files"
5. Click "Clear now"
6. Restart Edge and log in again

Safari (macOS):
1. Click Safari in the menu bar → Settings (or Preferences)
2. Go to the Privacy tab
3. Click "Manage Website Data" → "Remove All"
4. Go to the Develop menu → "Empty Caches" (enable Develop menu in Advanced settings if not visible)
5. Restart Safari and log in again

CLEARING COOKIES FOR A SPECIFIC SITE ONLY
-------------------------------------------
If you don't want to clear all cookies, you can clear them for our site only:

Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies → See all cookies → Search for "example.com" → Remove
Firefox: Settings → Privacy → Cookies → Manage Data → Search for "example.com" → Remove Selected
Edge: Settings → Cookies → See all cookies → Search for "example.com" → Remove

AFTER CLEARING
--------------
After clearing cookies and cache:
- You will need to log in again to all websites
- Saved preferences may be reset
- The first page load may be slightly slower as assets are re-downloaded
- If the issue persists after clearing, please contact support with:
  - Your browser name and version
  - Operating system
  - Screenshot of any error messages
  - Browser console output (press F12 → Console tab)

MOBILE BROWSER CACHE
---------------------
iOS Safari:
Settings → Safari → Clear History and Website Data

Android Chrome:
Chrome → Three-dot menu → History → Clear browsing data

Note: Clearing mobile browser data will also remove saved passwords 
unless you have them synced to your Google/Apple account.
